Rice milling,Production and Marketing in Nigeria.

Rice production and marketing has been one of the biggest businesses in Nigeria from time immemorial. And not only in Nigeria alone, we have countries like Thailand, India and China who are rice producing giants on a global scale. Picture yourself running a business that can be recognized and patronized on a global scale plus a loyal customer base that can grow to more than a billion patronizers over time.

Back to the Nigerian market scenery, out of the 36 states in the country we have only 10 states who are actively into rice production/farming and that is on paper. In reality we have less than that number actively producing rice. 70% of these mentioned states are all located in the northern part of the country, with the biggest rice milling companies all located in the same region

Rice milling and production as a lifetime business investment.

Rice milling and processing in Nigeria is at a very high demand due to constant demand for processed rice in the food market, which makes it a very lucrative venture for anyone ambitious enough to explore the food market sector. Nigeria is a country of over 300 million people and till date food has been a major problem for the masses due to low production, high consumption and poor government policies on food security in the economic front. Engaging in the production, distribution and marketing of the said commodity will bring in good returns on investment for investors who are willing to invest in this market.

It’s evident that Nigerians consume rice at a very high rate, a typical Nigerian eats rice 4-5 times in a week which puts rice on top of the chart for the most consumed food in the country. With a high rate of consumption comes great demand and that’s what we will leverage on to profit from the low supply of this food commodity.

GOOD LOCATION

The location chosen for the establishment of the proposed Rice mill plant is a town called Billiri, A Local Government Area located in Gombe state, Nigeria. Gombe state is a hot spot for Rice farming and production just like its three Neighboring states Bauchi State, Adamawa State, and Taraba state Respectively. In all these 4 mentioned states (Gombe state inclusive), there is no Standard Rice milling plant so everyone in this mentioned North Eastern states take their products to Kano state for proper milling, processing and marketing. If we can set up a plant in Billiri LGA, We will serve as a bridge between the three neighboring states and the Rice market on the national level, Hence creating competition for our Kano counterparts. Gombe state offers Stable/Steady Electricity, Cheap Labor and Low Cost in Land purchase and development of a rice milling plant .
